Calendar Setup in GHL:

  • Calendar Name: "IMPACT Leadership Assessment"
  • Duration: 60 minutes (recommended)
  • Buffer Time: 15 minutes before/after
  • Embed Type: Use GHL Calendar widget or iframe

Custom Form Fields (Configure in Calendar Settings):

  • Contact Information: First Name*, Last Name*, Email*, Phone Number*
  • Professional Context: Current Title* (Text), Company Name* (Text), LinkedIn Profile URL (Website)
  • Strategic Context:
    • "What is the #1 professional goal you are focused on achieving in the next 12 months?*" (Large Text Area)
    • "What is the single biggest challenge holding you back from achieving it?*" (Large Text Area)
  • Commitment & Readiness: "This process requires a significant investment of your time, energy, and financial resources to achieve the transformation you desire. Are you prepared to make that investment in yourself at this time?*" (Radio Buttons: "Yes, I am ready to invest" / "No, not at this time")

Calendar Settings:

  • Submit Button Text: "Schedule & Submit Application"
  • Post-Submission Redirect: application-received.html
  • Confirmation Email: Enable with professional messaging
  • Reminder Emails: 24 hours and 1 hour before appointment

Integration Steps:

  1. Create the calendar in your GHL account with the above specifications
  2. Configure the custom form fields in the calendar settings
  3. Get the calendar embed code from GHL
  4. Replace the placeholder iframe/script above with your actual GHL embed code
  5. Test the booking flow from selection to application-received.html
